Bio

American rock group formed in 1999 by guitarist Billy Howerdel and Tool lead vocalist Maynard James Keenan. Billy Howerdel looking to form the group, coincidently needed lodging, Kennan had a room in his N. Hollywood home and Billy moved in and the two of them became friends, and even though Billy originally invisioned a female on vocals the two of them formed A Perfect Circle with Keenan Lead Vocalist. Hits include; Powerless The Noose, Down and By, So Long and Thanks For The Fish
